Pahlo's Bio

I was born in Eugene, Oregon and raised in the Oregon Coastal Mountains in and around Elmira. I attended the Junction City School system for most of my formable years until I moved to Salem Oregon where I finish my last three years of High School at Gervais Union High School. After Graduation I attended the The University of Oregon. I continued my education while putting myself through school. It took 8 years, but I walked through ceremony in the Spring of 1996. A long the way I joined the Naval Reserve. A year after graduation I decided to return to active duty, but this time with the Marine Corps.

I have spent the past 20 years in the Marine Corps living in a variety of places to include:

  • Camp Pendleton, California
  • Twenty-nine Palms, California
  • Camp LeJuene, North Carolina
  • Brooklyn, New York
  • Kuwait
  • Iraq
  • Afghanistan
  • and my favorite Okinawa, Japan for the forth time.

I will retire next year and end with over 28 years of service. I intend on continuing in the Telecommunications field in the civilian work force.
